About Us

Our Purpose and Mission

Who we are

The Badger Hunting Club was founded in 2006 to unite hunters at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. Our mission is to unite the hunting community, promote and create new hunters, and to engage UW students in the importance and benefits of hunting.

What we offer

We educate members on hunting opportunities in the area, including available lands, regulations, as well as host speakers to inform us about hunting tips and tricks.

Aside from offering great opportunities for our members, we do our best to preserve the hunting heritage of America and specifically of Wisconsin. We are active in promoting and sponsoring the Learn to Hunt program for novice hunters, teaching hunter safety classes, and exposing non-hunters to the joys and benefits of hunting. We try to portray hunting in a different light, in order to break down some of the stereotypes people have about hunting by being active in habitat conservation, as well as viewing hunting as a means of acquiring sustainable and organic meat.

The club offers permissions from some land owners in the area to hunt, a place to get to know fellow hunters, gear for member use, as well as events of different purposes.

Learn to Hunt Program

In partnership with the Department of Natural Resources (DNR), the "Learn to Hunt" program offers beginner hunters an opportunity to learn from experienced mentors in a safe, controlled environment. Whether you’re interested in hunting deer, turkey, or small game, our program ensures you understand:

  • The basics of firearm safety and hunting regulations
  • How to scout and track wildlife
  • Field dressing and processing techniques

This program is perfect for those who are new to hunting or want to improve their skills with the guidance of seasoned experts. To join the next session, please visit our Learn to Hunt page at the Wisconsin DNR.

Resources & Education

Whether you’re new to hunting or looking to expand your skills, the Badger Hunting Club has a range of resources to help you succeed. From courses on gun safety to detailed guides on wildlife identification and field dressing techniques, we strive to make sure you feel confident and prepared.

  • Guided beginner hunting workshops
  • Wildlife tracking and identification classes
  • Field dressing and meat processing seminars
  • Conservation and wildlife management education
